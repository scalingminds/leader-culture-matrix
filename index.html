Culture + Performance Matrix

Where does each person land?

High performers who erode trust are the most dangerous people on a team. This matrix forces the honest conversation. Rate each team member on culture fit and performance, then decide what leadership requires of you.

Framework drawn from U.S. Navy SEAL evaluation criteria, popularized by Simon Sinek.

The Toxin
High Performance, Low Culture
Cut or transform — no exceptions
They hit their numbers but leave damage behind. Every day you keep them, you send a message to the rest of the team about what you actually value.
Mission-Ready
High Performance, High Culture
Protect, promote, and learn from
These people define what the team can become. They perform and they lift others. Know who they are and act accordingly — they have options.
Easy Decision
Low Performance, Low Culture
Let go — this one is clear
The hard part here is usually just admitting you already know the answer. Move quickly and with care. Slow exits are a kindness to no one.
Worth Coaching
Low Performance, High Culture
Invest — the foundation is there
They believe in what you're building and how you're building it. Performance gaps are fixable when the culture fit is real. Give them honest feedback and real support.
